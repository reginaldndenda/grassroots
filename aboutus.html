About Grassroots Cricket

Our Mission: To use the power of cricket to educate, inspire and mobilise communities.

GCI’s aim is to use the game of cricket as a medium for community development in Southern Africa in particular but also in Australia and other any countries where there is a demonstrated need.

The object of the organisation is to encourage the development of cricket in underdeveloped areas to provide an opportunity for all children but especially the underprivileged to enjoy a more fulfilling life.

Objectives

  1. To provide basic educational financial assistance to disadvantaged children.
  2. To provide financially and socially disadvantaged children with the opportunity to learn and play cricket.
  3. To provide coaches and equipment to participants
  4. To provide positive reinforcement during lessons in order to raise self esteem and self confidence
  5. To encourage participants to continue with their Cricket by joining a local club or by playing competition or social cricket.
  6. To promote a healthy lifestyle through regular active participation in the Grassroots Cricket Program.
